About

Fixed-Term Researcher at the Institute of Cognitive Sciences and Technologies (ISTC-CNR), Planning & Scheduling Technology Lab (Rome). I work at the intersection of robot-aided rehabilitation, cognitive/plan-based HRI, and multimodal user-state estimation (affect, workload, kinematics) to deliver personalized and adaptive interventions.

I design AI-driven control and monitoring pipelines with real-time feedback to adapt robot coaching during therapy and over long-term horizons.

Rehab robotics · Cognitive architectures · Automated planning · Multimodal monitoring
